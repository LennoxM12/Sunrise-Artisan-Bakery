About Us

Founded in 2018, Sunrise Artisan Bakery is a family-owned business based in Cape Town, dedicated to baking fresh, organic goods every day.

Our bakers blend traditional recipes with modern techniques to create breads, pastries, and cakes that bring joy to our customers. Every product is handmade with love and care.
